# Monthly Roundups

Monthly roundups are the lowest-friction way to keep the site fresh. Each roundup should collect a small set of papers, group them by theme, and end with a short synthesis of what changed in your understanding.

## Suggested Format

1. State the question or theme for the month.
2. Cover 3 to 5 papers or systems updates.
3. Give each item one paragraph: idea, why it matters, and one caution.
4. End with a synthesis section and links back to evergreen pages.

## Good Use Cases

- A wave of papers on the same topic.
- Several smaller updates that do not justify standalone posts.
- A month where the main value is comparison rather than deep critique.
